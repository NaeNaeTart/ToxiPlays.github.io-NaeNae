How to Use Pandora's Box

Welcome to Pandora's Box! This tool helps you organize and play your music albums with advanced features.

Getting Started

  • Create an Album: Click the hamburger menu (☰) and select "Create New Album".
  • Add Tracks: Open an album and click "Add Track" to upload audio files.
  • Edit Tracks: Click the edit button next to any track to modify metadata like producers, writers, tags, and mark as explicit.

Playback Features

  • Play Tracks: Click the play button on any track to start playback.
  • Controls: Use prev, next, shuffle, repeat, and volume controls.
  • Minimize Player: Click the minimize button (-) on the player to keep it playing in the background.

Advanced Features

  • Settings: Configure default producers/writers and more.
  • Tags: Add custom tags to tracks for better organization.
  • Download: Download individual tracks or entire albums as ZIP files.
  • Import/Export: Backup and restore your data.

Enjoy managing your music collection!
